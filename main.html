Audit fixes

What changed from the previous draft

  • Replaced company-by-company tabs with one integrated system map, because the ecosystem is cross-platform and shared by common rails.
  • Separated journey, money, physical fulfillment, data, and governance into different flow types instead of calling everything a money flow.
  • Removed unsupported take-rate and payout percentages, plus outdated or shaky product mappings, so the diagram stays defensible.
  • Added Thai public digital infrastructure and regulators that were previously missing: BOT, PromptPay, PromptBiz, ETDA DPS, PDPC, NBTC, DGA GDX, and NDID.

  9. TikTok Newsroom Thailand and TikTok Southeast Asia update - used for the Thailand monthly-user figure and TikTok's role in Thai digital commerce and discovery.

This map intentionally avoids exact fee splits, take rates, or platform-specific payout percentages unless they are part of the cited public infrastructure or first-party disclosures. The goal is factual structure first, not speculative precision.
